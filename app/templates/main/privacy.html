隐私政策

最后更新：2026年2月6日

1. 简介

LoL Performance Analyzer（以下简称“我们”）致力于保护你的隐私。本隐私政策说明你在使用我们的网页平台与分析服务时，我们如何收集、使用和保护你的信息。

2. 我们收集的信息

你主动提供的信息

  • 邮箱地址（用于账号注册和登录）
  • 英雄联盟召唤师名称与标签（用于对局分析）
  • Discord 频道 ID（用于通知投递）

自动收集的信息

  • Riot Games API 数据：对局统计、对局行为数据和表现指标
  • 对局分析结果与生成的建议
  • 服务使用统计与错误日志

3. 我们如何使用这些信息

我们使用收集到的信息用于：

  • 提供对局分析与表现建议
  • 发送自动化 Discord 对局通知
  • 在仪表盘展示对局历史与表现趋势
  • 生成每周表现总结
  • 改进分析算法
  • 维护并改进本服务

4. 数据存储与安全

  • 账号数据：邮箱与密码哈希将安全存储在数据库中
  • 对局数据：分析结果会被存储，用于历史查看与周报
  • 密码：仅以加密哈希形式存储，绝不保存明文
  • 不向第三方出售：我们不会出售或共享你的个人数据给第三方

5. 数据保留

  • 账号数据将保留至你删除账号为止
  • 对局分析数据将保留用于仪表盘历史记录
  • 你可随时请求删除全部数据

6. 你的权利

你有权：

  • 通过仪表盘访问个人数据
  • 请求删除账号与关联数据
  • 随时关闭 Discord 通知
  • 导出你的对局分析数据

7. 儿童隐私

本服务不面向 13 岁以下用户。我们不会有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

8. 第三方服务

本服务集成了以下第三方：

  • Riot Games API：用于获取英雄联盟对局数据（受 Riot 隐私政策约束）
  • Discord API：用于通知投递（受 Discord 隐私政策约束）

我们不对这些第三方服务的隐私实践负责。

9. 本政策的变更

我们可能会不定期更新本隐私政策。若有重大变更，我们会更新“最后更新”日期并通过服务内通知告知用户。

10. 联系我们

如你对本隐私政策有疑问，请联系：

  • GitHub：github.com/yd1008/lol-analyzer
  • 开发者：Elliot Dang（yd1008@nyu.edu）

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

LoL Performance Analyzer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web platform and analysis service.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • League of Legends summoner name and tagline (for match analysis)
  • Discord channel ID (for notification delivery)

Information Automatically Collected

  • Riot Games API data: match statistics, gameplay data, and performance metrics
  • Match analysis results and generated recommendations
  • Service usage statistics and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide match analysis and performance recommendations
  • Send automated Discord notifications with match reports
  • Display match history and performance trends on your dashboard
  • Generate weekly performance summaries
  • Improve our analysis algorithms
  • Maintain and improve the Service

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Account data: Email and password hash stored securely in our database
  • Match data: Match analysis results are stored to enable dashboard history and weekly summaries
  • Passwords: Stored only as cryptographic hashes, never in plain text
  • No third-party sharing: We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Match analysis data is retained for your dashboard history
  • You may request deletion of all your data at any time

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the dashboard
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out of Discord notifications at any time
  • Export your match analysis data

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with:

  • Riot Games API: For League of Legends match data (governed by Riot's Privacy Policy)
  • Discord API: For notification delivery (governed by Discord's Privacy Policy)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and providing notice through the Service.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

  • GitHub: github.com/yd1008/lol-analyzer
  • Developer: Elliot Dang (yd1008@nyu.edu)
